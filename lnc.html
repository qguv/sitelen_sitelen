Reflection: Language and Culture

Quint Guvernator

Our section on research ethics, specifically with respect to linguistic gratuity and research debt encurred, really resonated with me. I feel strongly that specialists in any field ought to use their skills to try to improve quality of life however they can.

I have been interested in updating and improving the linguist's toolbox ever since I first considered combining linguistics and computer science. For me, a big part of language and culture is the ability to recognize the linguistic needs of a community.

In lieu of a purely reflective piece, I have tried to put into action (in a very small way) my ideals by creating an experimental piece of software to properly render a particularly challenging class of script. Please see the project description for more details.
